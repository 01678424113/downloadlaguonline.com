Michael Robert Henrion "Mike" Posner (/ˈpoʊznər/ pohz-nər; born February 12, 1988) is an American singer, songwriter and record producer. Posner released his debut album, 31 Minutes to Takeoff, on August 10, 2010. The album includes the US Billboard Hot 100 top 10 single "Cooler than Me" as well as the top 20 single "Please Don't Go". In 2016, a remix of his 2015 single "I Took a Pill in Ibiza" peaked in the top ten on the charts in twenty seven countries around the world, including hitting number one in many and the top 5 on the Billboard Hot 100 in the United States. He is also known for co-writing "Sugar" by Maroon 5, "Boyfriend" by Justin Bieber, "Beneath Your Beautiful" by Labrinth, as well as songs for Talib Kweli, Pharrell Williams, Big Sean, 2 Chainz, Wiz Khalifa, Nelly, TI, Snoop Dogg, Nick Jonas, Avicii, Austin Mahone, and more.
